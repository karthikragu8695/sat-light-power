WHO WE ARE

SAT LIGHT AND POWER is a EPC company specializing in Solar mini mast, LED mini mast, High Mast Projects, Solar roof top, Solar street lights , LED street light projects.

We Work with All Government Projects, Panchayat Projects, Corporation Projects, Private Projects.

We have on rich experience in Since 2010.

SAT LIGHTS AND POWER was formed in the year 2019 by Entrepreneurs who have rich experience in Renewable Energy Background.
